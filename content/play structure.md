# SESSION START

## first...
- if beginning from a fresh start in the narrative...
	- ### BEGIN A SESSION (iron vault)
		roll the move w iron vault & envision what the prompt might mean
	- ### TALES FROM THE DRIFT
		for further inspiration, roll up to 3 oracles, dealer's choice, & warm up with a flash fiction vignette that relates to the main campaign in some way 
	- ### LAST TIME ON...
		write a short summary of the previous narrative arc with a focus on the last session
## or...
- if beginning from a pause in an active arc...
	- ### TALES FROM THE DRIFT
		roll up to 3 oracles, dealer's choice, & warm up with a flash fiction vignette about events in the larger galaxy with no major ties to the main campaign (that you know of...)
	- ### LAST TIME ON...
		write a short summary of the current arc with a focus on the last session
## then...
- ### SET THE STAGE
	envision where the main characters are and what they might be doing. if unsure, roll on the fate chart or use an oracle. write a note about your expectations for the scene.
- ### SET THE SCENE
	use the mythic shortcuts to begin a new scene.
		*if things are beginning from a point of narrative stability, reset the scene counter. otherwise, reset the counter and manually adjust the chaos factor based on the end of the last session.*
- ### PLAY THE GAME
	write down what's happening. if the scene was altered or interrupted, go with the flow. roll oracles or ask fate questions when necessary. when the fiction dictates you make a move, make the appropriate move (iron vault). reference the rules and tools as needed. 
- ### RINSE & REPEAT
	write, ask the oracle, and make moves until the scene or session reaches a stopping point that you're satisfied with. write ooc notes to track your thought processes to make it easier to look over and remind yourself of what you were thinking, but don't let it get in the way of the fun.
		*if you reach the end of a scene before the end of a session, which is likely, simply note down your expectations for the next scene, use mythic to progress, & keep playing*

# SESSION END

## upkeep
- make changes to your mythic lists as necessary. 
- save your state with ;;state save **(do not forget this part!!)**
## wind down
- use the end a session move. reflect on how you're feeling. write some notes on your thoughts and/or any ideas you might have for next time. leave yourself some breadcrumbs.
